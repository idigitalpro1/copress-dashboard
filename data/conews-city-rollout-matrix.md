## Per-Town Launch Checklist

1. Town landing page with title, meta description, and JSON-LD `Organization`.
2. Weekly edition shell with front page, follow-ups, sports/events, Shop Local, and sponsor strip.
3. Shop Local seed list with at least 20 verified businesses or a clear research placeholder.
4. Events calendar source list with meeting/event links.
5. Author lane assignment for Patrick, Joathon, and Paul.
6. Contributor upload link and onboarding CTA.
7. Sponsor CTA and intake form link.
8. SATCOM dashboard card with thumbnail, status, blockers, and next action.
