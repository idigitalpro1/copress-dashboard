# Operator One-Sheet

## CoPress Nest

One command center for local publishing, advertiser campaigns, newsletters, billing handoff, city-site routing, and operator support.

### What It Does

- Turns a sales brief into a campaign kit.
- Sends campaign copy into Newsletter Studio.
- Routes billing work to invoice manager.
- Stages city-site and directory intake.
- Keeps editorial queues, publish targets, and production handoff visible.
- Gives Nest/Hermes a clear operator lane instead of a scattered chat.

### Best Use

Use this when a publisher, advertiser, sponsor, city-site operator, or internal sales rep needs a fast campaign package with visible assets and a production path.

### Guardrail

The dashboard stages and hands off work. External writes, billing, DNS, and production deploys remain gated through dedicated tools and operator approval.
