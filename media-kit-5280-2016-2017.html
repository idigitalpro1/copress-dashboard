About
Who We Are

5280 Magazine is Denver's premier lifestyle publication — a monthly (with monthly supplements) guide to the extraordinary people, places, restaurants, real estate, and culture that define the Mile High City. Published in print and digital, 5280 reaches affluent, engaged readers who live, work, and play in the Denver metro area.

With award-winning journalism including our 2016 Newsmaker of the Year honor and multiple Rocky Mountain News Publications Competition wins, 5280 doesn't just cover Denver — we shape the conversation. Our editorial team brings world-class reporting to every story: restaurant reviews that move diners to action, real estate profiles that move the market, and cultural coverage that defines the city's identity.

Founded by Darryl Peters, 5280 has been named to the American Journalism Foundation's list of "Best Practices" publications — the only regional city magazine to receive that honor. Our advertisers benefit from a publication trusted by its audience and respected across the industry.

"The only regional city magazine named to the American Journalism Foundation's 'Best Practices' list."
