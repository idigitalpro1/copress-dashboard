Rick's Cabaret Central City

Late-night steakhouse, lounge, and cabaret energy minutes from Black Hawk.

Start upstairs with steakhouse reservations, fine wines, champagne, and booth seating. Keep the night moving downstairs with lounge energy and two floors of cabaret entertainment.
