# StansAuto.5280.menu Placement Pricing

## Monthly Plan

Unlimited Queue - $50/month

Meaning: the advertiser can send unlimited ideas/assets into the CoMSAT queue, and CoMSAT publishes up to 10 approved posts per month.

Included post types:
- Sponsored editorial post
- Social media card/post
- Banner placement
- Newsletter block
- Landing-page update
- Radio/audio script placement
- Print/direct mail asset

Approval rule:
All content runs through SATCOM review before placement. Vehicle availability, mileage, price, financing, warranty, and specific inventory claims require same-day verification.

## CoMSAT Flow

1. Intake request enters SATCOM/CoMSAT.
2. Operator selects monthly plan or menu placement.
3. Asset cards in the owner-facing kit open the shopping-cart info drawer.
4. Selected asset links attach to the kanban card.
5. Claim check is completed.
6. Approved item is placed immediately or scheduled inside the monthly 10-post allowance.
